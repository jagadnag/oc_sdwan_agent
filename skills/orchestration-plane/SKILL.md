# Orchestration Plane

## Overview
The orchestration plane manages SD-WAN fabric provisioning, configuration, and lifecycle management.

## Components
- **vManage:** Management and monitoring
- **vBond:** Orchestration and onboarding
- **vSmart:** Control plane
- **vEdge/cEdge:** Data plane devices

## Key Functions
- Device provisioning and onboarding
- Configuration management
- Policy deployment
- Monitoring and analytics
- Troubleshooting support

## Operations
- Device registration
- Certificate management
- Configuration backup/restore
- Policy updates
- Performance monitoring

## Best Practices
- Automate provisioning
- Maintain redundant controllers
- Regular backup of management data
- Monitor controller health
- Plan controller capacity
